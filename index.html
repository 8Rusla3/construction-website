Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How quickly do you respond to emergency calls? ▼

We aim to respond to emergency drain issues within 2-4 hours during business hours. We also offer 24/7 emergency services—call our main line for after-hours emergencies.

Do you provide free estimates? ▼

Yes! We provide free on-site estimates with transparent pricing. No hidden fees, no surprises. We'll give you a clear scope, timeline, and cost before any work begins.

What areas do you service? ▼

We serve Mississauga, Toronto, and the Greater Toronto Area. If you're unsure if your location is covered, give us a call at +1 437-214-3053.

How long does a typical drain repair take? ▼

Most drain repairs take 2-6 hours depending on complexity. Simple clogs might take 1-2 hours, while more involved work like drain replacement could take a full day. We'll provide exact timing during the estimate.

Are you licensed and insured? ▼

Yes! Centaurus Constructions is fully licensed and insured. We carry comprehensive liability and workers' compensation coverage to protect you and your property.

Do you offer financing or payment plans? ▼

We accept all major credit cards and cash. For larger projects, we can discuss flexible payment options—contact us to learn more.
